Our Story

"Beauty begins the moment you decide to be yourself."

Crafting Perfection Since 2021

At Bonnie Nails, we believe that luxury should be an everyday experience. Founded by master nail artist Ngughii Bonnie, our salon brings Parisian elegance to modern nail care.

We meticulously select only the finest products that are kind to your nails and the environment. Our commitment to excellence has earned us recognition as one of the top nail studios in the region.

Every visit to our sanctuary is designed to be a moment of tranquility and transformation, where expert craftsmanship meets personalized attention.
